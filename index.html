Tournament planner

Minutes per player (all 5 games)

Each half = 12:30. Red = 0 min that game. Orange = total < 50 min. Green = ≥ 60 min. GK = goalkeeper that game (full game); GK·H1 / GK·H2 = goalkeeper one half only. Above the games: player pills use a continuous red-yellow-green gradient based on % of fair share (deep red < 50%, yellow = on target, deep green > 140%). Pills are sorted ascending by minutes so players needing more time appear first. The percentage next to each player is their current share vs. expected. Click a pill to highlight every appearance of that player across all games. Click more pills to add to the selection; click a highlighted pill again to remove, or hit Clear. Tap a chip, then tap another chip in the same game, to swap (mobile-friendly alternative to drag-drop). Tap the same chip twice to deselect. A small ⚙ gear appears on the right of each position's label — click it to reveal merge/split buttons for that spot. Merged positions get Split; divided positions get Keep [starter] / Keep [sub]. A player benched both halves of a game (not playing) is highlighted in red on both of their bench chips inside the affected game. On the pitch, a chip gets a red border if its player is in a divided position here but is also on the field in the other half elsewhere (i.e. the "sub" is actually just a position switch, not a real substitution). Use the Versions ▾ button to save the current plan under a name and reload it later; each game's current state is always auto-saved separately from named versions. Drag any chip to any slot within the same game to reorganize.
